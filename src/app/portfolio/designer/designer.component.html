Low-code platform

On this project, we rebuilt our old program into a new flagship design software. A complex and but easy to use product that is available in three editions. The same software had to cover the needs of a wide spectrum of B2B businesses, from small bakery shops to multinational corporations. The software also offers low-code, but powerful application builder. I was the lead front-end programmer. I architectured the front-end layer and instructed other programmers on how to program and work with WPF desktop technology.
